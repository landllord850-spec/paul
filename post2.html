City Evenings: Where To Eat Like A Local

Late-night markets, small plates, and quiet corners

City nights are best explored slowly. Try food stalls that have a steady local line, look for menus in the local language, and don't be afraid to point and ask. Markets frequently open after sunset and are great for sampling small bites without breaking the bank.

Tip: carry small change and a napkin. Enjoy the walk — many great results come simply by following where locals gather.
