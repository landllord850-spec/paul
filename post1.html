Sunset Beaches and Quiet Villages

Finding peaceful coasts and local stays

We took a small coastal route away from popular boards and discovered charming villages, family-run guesthouses, and empty sunsets. Bring layers — evenings can be cool even after warm days. Look for pension signs, ask locals for small cafés, and keep an eye out for local markets at dusk.

Packing tip: a lightweight towel, a small headlamp, and an extra pair of shoes you don't mind getting sandy will make beach-hopping easy and relaxed.
